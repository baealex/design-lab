Inspired by Material 3 Expressive

Material 3Expressive.

Expression becomes useful when color, shape, size, motion, and type make the next action easier to find.

Context

Material You made interfaces personal through dynamic color. Material 3 Expressive extends that foundation after research that began in 2022, asking a different question: can an interface feel more distinct while becoming easier to use? Its answer is not more decoration. It is stronger hierarchy, responsive components, and motion that preserves relationships.

Our take

What we keep / What we leave behind

What we keep

  • Size and containment that make the primary action faster to find.
  • Shape and type changes tied to real state transitions.
  • Spring motion that preserves spatial relationships.
  • Material You color roles as a stable personalization foundation.

What we leave behind

  • Expressive shapes on every surface, even when they communicate nothing.
  • Unfamiliar silhouettes that replace clear labels or familiar patterns.
  • Motion that adds spectacle without explaining a state change.
  • Copying Google’s visual identity instead of adapting the principles to the product.
